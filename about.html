Founders

Anshuman Patil

"We noticed that popular perfumes can be way too expensive, so we started eaudihfragrance to bring high-quality fragrances within reach for everyone, without compromising on what makes them special."

As a high school student passionate about fragrances, Anshuman co-founded eaudihfragrance to make luxury fragrances more accessible, combining his love for perfumes with a commitment to fair pricing.

Aryaman Chavan

"We wanted to share our passion for fragrances and make luxury perfumes approachable for everyone, without losing the quality and experience that make them unique."

Also a high school student, Aryaman co-founded eaudihfragrance along with Anshuman, bringing energy, creativity, and a shared love for perfumes to help others enjoy their favorite scents at fair prices.
